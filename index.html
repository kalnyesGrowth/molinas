FAQ

Common Questions

How fast can you turn over a property?

We clean inside the standard 11-to-4 window. Same-day turnovers are our normal, not our exception. For properties over 4 bedrooms, we send a two-person team to stay within the window.

What if my regular cleaner is sick?

A backup cleaner is assigned to every property and already knows your checklist. You never feel the difference. No-shows and call-outs are our problem, never yours.

Do you handle linens and laundry?

Yes, on Premium Turnovers or as a $45 add-on to any clean. We wash, dry, fold, and swap. Fresh linens are staged hotel-style for every guest arrival.

Will you restock supplies?

Yes. We track what each property needs and restock consumables (toilet paper, soap, coffee pods, trash bags) so guests never hit an empty roll.

How do I know the clean was actually done?

30+ timestamped photos after every turnover, sent straight to your phone. Every room, every surface, every detail documented. You see it before your guest does.

Do you work with property managers?

Yes. Whether you manage one unit or two hundred, we scale to your portfolio with one point of contact, consolidated invoicing, and priority scheduling.

What areas do you cover?

Naples, Marco Island, Bonita Springs, Fort Myers Beach, Orlando, Kissimmee, Davenport, ChampionsGate, and surrounding short-term rental markets. Send your address and we'll confirm coverage.

How do I get pricing?

Request a quote with your property details (address, beds, baths, frequency). You get a flat per-turnover rate with no surprises, no hidden fees, no surge pricing.
